What's next?

Consultation

On your consultation, we get to meet each other and chat about what you want from the treatment. I'll talk you through the styles and shades that might suit you, and we go through your paperwork and medical history.

Patch Test

Sometimes, we can carry out your treatment straight after your consultation. If you'd prefer to book in separately, after a patch test, that's fine too.

Treatment Day

Treatment day starts with a quick re-cap of our consultation followed by a mapping session. This is where I map on your brows and mark them up so we can see what they'll look like once tattooed.

We then select shades based on your skin tones and get to work.

 The Procedure

After we've applied numbing cream and got you comfortable, we'll select shades and map your brows. The whole treatment will take around 2 hours from start to finish.

 The Re-Touch

6-8 weeks after your initial treatment, we'll meet again for the re-touch. Semi Permanent Makeup is a multi-treatment procedure, and the Re-Touch (or, Top-Up) is necessary to adjust and finalise the treatment.
